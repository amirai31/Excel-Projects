One specific area of analysis involves examining store sales over a year to gain insights and develop strategies for improvement in the upcoming year.

Sharing the store analysis dashboard developed in Excel.

After the store analysis, insights are:
- focus on popular e-commerce platforms such as Amazon, Myntra, and Flipkart for promotional campaigns, especially during the month of March
- capture the attention and interest of women customers in the age group, 21-39 residing in the states - Maharashtra and Karnataka
- direct promotional efforts towards the leading category, 'set', to maximize sales
- enhance the delivery success rate, currently at 93%, to achieve a higher percentage of items successfully delivered

By implementing these recommendations, businesses can optimize their marketing strategies, increase visibility among the identified demographic, and ultimately drive sales growth in the coming year.
